THE 9 BEST INTERACTIVE 404 PAGE EXAMPLES

Error pages don’t have to be boring. Make your brand stand out by building them with Rive.

Alex Morgan
Oct 24, 2024
8 min read
Interface Design Motion

We’ve all seen ‘em, boring, bare-bones 404 error pages with little more than a “Page Not Found” message on them. But it doesn’t have to be that way. Instead of looking like an afterthought, 404 pages can be surprisingly fun and memorable if you think of them as a way to delight visitors and leave a lasting impression of your brand and personality. That’s where Rive comes in. Here are nine custom 404 pages built with Rive, each one expressive, entertaining, and interactive in its own beautiful, clever, weird, or silly way. Many of these files are in the Rive Marketplace and remixable in Rive, letting you learn from other creators. Check these out and discover how to use Rive to turn a mistake into something people will remember —and maybe even snort-laugh over.

Delight in broken link by Abe Yang

Mice under construction by Dowski

Who jumps the void by Laura Calvo

Ice cream by Juan Carlos Cruz

Hidden hover by Yuji Ishikawa

Moon by kaaveh

Cow + UFO by Frans Bergström

Fire 404 by Pedro Alpera

Astronaut by ARYAY

So what are you waiting for? Start building your own unique error pages with Rive. We’ve even got a fun Speedrun 404 tutorial by thelittlelabs to get you started. With Rive, there’s no such thing as a dead-end white page that says “Oops!”
